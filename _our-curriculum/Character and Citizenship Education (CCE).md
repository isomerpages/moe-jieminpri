<p>At Jiemin Primary School, Character and Citizenship Education (CCE) forms
the heart of our efforts in nurturing students who are confident in their
learning, caring and being responsible members of our society. Guided by
our school’s vision – Global Learners, Discerning Citizens and our mission
– Nurturing Hearts, Nurturing Minds, CCE empowers our students to thrive
with our values and purpose in an ever-changing world.</p>
<p>Our CCE programmes offer a holistic and engaging journey. It is designed
to cultivate strong character, a deep sense of civic responsibility, and
a proud national identity in our students. Grounded in the Ministry of
Education's CCE 2021 Curriculum Frame (as illustrated in Figure 1), our
programmes are strategically aligned with MOE’s goals of helping students
internalise core values, develop crucial social-emotional competencies,
and prepare for the evolving demands of a rapidly changing world, so that
they can become active contributors to the community.</p>
