<p>At Jiemin Primary School, Character and Citizenship Education (CCE) forms
the heart of our efforts in nurturing students who are confident in their
learning, caring and being responsible members of our society. Guided by
our school’s vision – Global Learners, Discerning Citizens and our mission
– Nurturing Hearts, Nurturing Minds, CCE empowers our students to thrive
with our values and purpose in an ever-changing world.</p>
<p>Our CCE programmes offer a holistic and engaging journey. It is designed
to cultivate strong character, a deep sense of civic responsibility, and
a proud national identity in our students. Grounded in the Ministry of
Education's CCE 2021 Curriculum Frame (as illustrated in Figure 1), our
programmes are strategically aligned with MOE’s goals of helping students
internalise core values, develop crucial social-emotional competencies,
and prepare for the evolving demands of a rapidly changing world, so that
they can become active contributors to the community.</p>
<p>We intentionally cultivate the Jiemin core values: Resilience, Responsibility,
Respect, Humility, Care. These values are infused across classroom interactions,
school-wide initiatives, and real-life experiences that shape our students’
identity and character. From the everyday interactions in the classroom
to impactful school-wide initiatives, each experience is thoughtfully designed
to nurture growth in both their hearts and minds.</p>

<h4><strong>Key Highlights of our CCE Programme:</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Building Character Through Everyday Experiences:</strong> 
</p>
<p>We believe that character development is an ongoing process. Our daily
classroom routines and interactions are infused with opportunities for
students to practice and internalize our core values.</p>
<p><strong>Experiential Learning Through Cohort Learning Journeys (LJs):</strong> 
</p>
<p>We provide enriching out-of-classroom experiences that bring learning
to life and deepen understanding of our heritage and society.</p>
<p><strong>Empowering Student Leadership and Kindness:</strong> 
</p>
<p>Our Prefects and Peer Support Leaders drive the Kindness Movement, actively
promoting a positive and supportive school environment and encouraging
empathy and care among students.</p>
