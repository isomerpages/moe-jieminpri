<p>The Values in Action (VIA) programme in Jiemin Primary School (JPS) is
conceptualised to achieve the school’s vision of Global Learners, Discerning
Citizens. The school core values of Responsibilities, Resilience, Respect
Humility and Care is reinforced through the various opportunities for students
to practise them consciously at home, in school and within the community.</p>
<p>&nbsp;The VIA programme is designed to help students acquire the 21st
century competencies and skills as outlined in the MOE’s Framework for
21st Century Competencies and Student Outcomes. Through the planning and
implementation of the VIA programmes/activities, students are exposed to
the learning experiences that supports their development as socially responsible
citizens who contribute meaningfully to the home, school and community.
It also helps shape the students' character and provides a holistic education
beyond the classroom.</p>
<p>Students are guided by the <strong>3As Approach “Awareness- Action- Advocacy”,</strong> which
aims to strengthen students’ engagement and provide them with a meaningful
learning experience while working on their VIA Projects.</p>
