<h2><strong>Peer Support @ JPS 2025</strong></h2>
<p></p>
<p>Peer Support Relationships (PSR) initiative builds on our school’s culture
of care and aims to create a caring and enabling environment in Jiemin
Primary School. The school leverages on our Peer Support Leaders to strengthen
positive pro-social and support network through <strong>Peer Bonding</strong>, <strong>Peer Helping</strong> and <strong>Peer Influencing</strong>.</p>

<h4><strong>Peer Support Leaders (PSLs)</strong></h4>
<p>We believe that every Jieminite can be a Peer Supporter. All Jieminites
will be equipped with the basic PSR knowledge and skills once a term. However,
a selected group of Student Leaders known as Peer Support Leaders are further
trained to contribute to PSR initiatives. They are appointed from Primary
3 onwards and their appointments are recognized during the <strong>Student Leaders Investiture</strong> in
Term 2.</p>

<h4><strong>1. Peer Bonding</strong></h4>
<p>In our efforts to foster bonds and sense of belonging in Jiemin Primary
School, various initiatives are being conducted by the PSLs. They help
to orientate new Jieminites during our <strong>P1 Orientation</strong>.
They also manage a structured play programme during recess called <strong>PLAY</strong> (<strong>P</strong>atience, <strong>L</strong>isten, <strong>A</strong>ppreciate, <strong>Y</strong>ay!)@Jiemin.
Playing a variety of games with their peers enable them to strengthen bonds
and encourage 21 CC Competencies such as communication and collaboration
skills. PSLs undergo <strong>training sessions</strong> at least once a term
where they practise teamwork as they solve challenges together.</p>
