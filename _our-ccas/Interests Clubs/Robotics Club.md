<p>The Robotics Club gather pupils who have a strong interest in various
types of robotics games, are interested in learning robot programming and
enjoy the excitement during competition. The main coding programme that
they learn to use is Scratch. They will be engaged in different coding
projects and get to showcase their works in school.</p>
