<p>In Journalist Club, pupils pick up various journalistic skills such as
photography, photojournalism, interview and writing skills. They are given
a variety of opportunities to hone these skills, such as working on school
events as reporters and photographers. They strive to conduct accurate
interviews and produce compelling and creative photographs with each opportunity.</p>
