<p>Jieminites will build a strong and healthy body, and develop the virtues
of modesty and perseverance through Taekwondo.
<br>Instructors will teach them effective self defence techniques. Through
the training, members also cultivate tolerance, self-discipline and respect.
<br>Our students get opportunities to take part in national and community
TKD competitions (Poomsae).</p>
